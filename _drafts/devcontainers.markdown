### Machines inside machines
<!--more-->

Technology can be challenging. With so many moving parts and dependencies, any one of many small things changing could mean a failure of some sort. When doing development, managing these to ensure stability throughtout the development process and then, especially, in production is an important part of the process. One modern approach to solving part of this problem is the devcontainer. But before we get to that, we need to understand containers.
